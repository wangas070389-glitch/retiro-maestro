# Mission Statement: Retiro Maestro — Product & Monetization (MARCH 2026)

## 🎯 Strategic Objective
To provide a best-in-class pension calculation and optimization platform for individual users (B2C) and professional advisors (B2B) by transforming raw data into clear, actionable financial decisions. Retiro Maestro aims to become the standard infrastructure for IMSS Ley 73 validation in Mexico.

---

## 🛠️ Operational Objectives (Functional Scope)

### 1. B2C Growth & Conversion
*   **Progressive Disclosure UI**: Direct the user from basic math to high-value decision-making.
*   **Result (Hero) Block**: Immediately display the base pension with an attractive upsell to maximize it.
*   **Tiered Monetization**:
    *   **Free**: Basic simulation.
    *   **Estrategia Completa ($590)**: Comparative analysis and ROI.
    *   **Dossier Profesional ($1,490)**: Certified PDF with SHA-256 hash.

### 2. B2B Advisor Ecosystem
*   **Subscription Management**: Starter ($990), Growth ($1,990), and Pro ($3,990) tiers based on client volume.
*   **Advisor Dashboard**: Manage multiple clients, save simulations, and generate strategy cards.
*   **Branding (Marca Blanca)**: Enable advisors to add their own logos and contact info to generated PDFs (Pro tier).

### 3. Core Strategy Engine
*   **Recommendation Executive Block**: "Qué hacer, cuánto invertir, cuánto ganar, y en cuánto tiempo."
*   **Optimizer (Server-side)**: Perform multi-scenario simulations (ROI, Max Pension) exclusively on the server for sovereignty.
*   **Inverse Design**: Calculate the necessary investment for a target monthly pension.

### 4. Legal & Authority Layer
*   **Centro de Control Legal**: Centralized access to the "Digital Expediente," Payment Calendar, and Oracle documents.
*   **Official Parity**: Reports must match IMSS visual authority and terminology.

---

## 📉 Success Metrics
*   **Clarity**: Users can confirm "Ya sé qué hacer con mi pensión" after one session.
*   **Revenue**: Conversion rate from Free to Paid (B2C) and recurring ARPU (B2B).
*   **Retention**: Low churn rate for advisors on subscription plans.

---

## 🛑 Anti-Objectives
*   **NO** calculation logic exposed in the client-side code (Server Action Only).
*   **NO** "data-dump" reports; all reports must lead to an actionable decision.
*   **NO** manual intervention in calculations (Math must be self-verifying).
