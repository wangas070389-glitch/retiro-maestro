# Mission Statement: Sovereign Closing Machine (v3.0.0-terminal)

## Mission
To **Maximizing Conversion & Business Closure** for **Retiro Maestro Advisors** by providing a **High-Authority Closing Terminal** that transforms actuarial math into emotional urgency and actionable CRM data.

---

## Functional Scope (Must-Have for Survival)

### 📈 Closing Intelligence (The "Aha" Moment)
- **Costo de Demora**: Displaying the monthly and lifetime cost of NOT acting immediately (Opportunity Loss).
- **Inertial vs. Strategy Mapping**: Visualizing the financial gap between "Doing nothing" and "The Plan."
- **Executive Recommendation**: Intelligent identification of the "Winning Scenario" (Top Investment / ROI).

### 🕹️ Interactive Workstation (The "Closing Tool")
- **Live Simulator**: Reactive sliders for M40 Years, Average Salary, and Retire Age (Instant Re-calculation).
- **Strategy Selection**: One-click "Lock-in" of a chosen scenario as the client's permanent roadmap.

### 🧾 Sovereign CRM (The "Business Engine")
- **Sales Pipeline**: Status management (Prospect -> Analyzing -> Proposal Sent -> Closed).
- **Contextual Narrative**: Generation of personalized sales scripts ("Argumentario") based on simulation results.
- **Unified History**: Persistent notes and interaction logs per client.

### 🚀 Action & Conversion
- **WhatsApp Integration**: Single-click proposal sharing.
- **Decision Timeline**: Step-by-step visual roadmap from current status to first pension payment.

---

## Anti-Objectives (Scope Guardrails)
- **NO External Lead Hubs**: This terminal is a self-contained Sovereign ecosystem.
- **NO Generic Advice**: Every insight must be grounded in valid IMSS/SINDO evidence.
- **NO Passive UI**: The interface must actively prompt the advisor for the "Next Step."

---

## Success Metric
Advisors closing **30% more plans** due to the emotional impact of "Costo de Demora" and the clarity of the "Action Roadmap."
