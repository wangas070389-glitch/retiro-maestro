# 🛡️ Red Team Analysis: Retiro Maestro

## Anti-Objectives
*   **Actuarial Inaccuracy**: Delivering a projection that differs by >0.1% from the actual IMSS resolution due to logic errors.
*   **Legal Drift**: Providing advice that leads to a smaller pension due to misunderstanding Article 25 (Modalidad 40) caps.
*   **Financial Ruin**: Calculating a ROI for Modalidad 40 that ignores inflation (INPC) or regional salary updates, misleading the worker.

## Pre-Mortem Narrative
"It is February 2027, and 'Retiro Maestro' has been shut down following several legal complaints from users. The failure wasn't in the calculations themselves, but in **Data Integrity**. Users uploaded IMSS statements that the parser misread—swapping 'Weeks' and 'Salary' fields. Because the system didn't implement **Protocol 17 (Sentinel)** for cross-verification of manual input against OCR data, users over-invested in Modalidad 40 based on hallucinated weeks, suffering catastrophic financial loss."
