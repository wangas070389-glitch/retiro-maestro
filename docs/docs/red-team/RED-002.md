# 🛡️ Red Team Analysis: Interactive Simulation Platform

## Anti-Objectives
*   **Misleading Enrichment**: Suggesting a Modalidad 40 investment that nets a negative lifetime ROI due to administrative fees or inflation gaps.
*   **Data Desync**: Presenting a "optimized" scenario that uses inconsistent salary anchors (UMA vs Pesos) compared to the base scenario.
*   **False Confidence**: Providing projections for age 65 without a clear "Legal Validity" disclaimer for workers with interrupted rights (Conservación de Derechos).

## Pre-Mortem Narrative
"The launch of the interactive twin was a technical success but an actuarial failure. Users began creating 'Aggressive Investment' scenarios where they input salaries higher than the legal cap of 25 UMAs. Because the UI didn't enforce the **Actuarial Factor Capping** logic at the edge (Input Layer), the resulting graphs showed millionaire pensions that were legally impossible. Users based their current spending habits on these 'optimized' ghosts, leading to a wave of financial distress when the IMSS resolutions arrived at half the predicted value."

## Lindy Effect Audit
*   **Visualization Logic**: standard D3 primitives (High Lindy).
*   **State Management**: immutable Redux-style transitions (Durable).
*   **Cap Logic**: Server-side enforcement required to mitigate UI-layer manipulation.
