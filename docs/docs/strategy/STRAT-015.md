# STRAT-015: Radical Transparency (Analysis Module)

## 1. Vision
**"Trust through Verification."**
The user should not just accept the "Monthly Pension" number. They must understand *how* it evolves over time. The Deep Analysis Module provides a granular, year-by-year breakdown of the strategy, visualizing the "Pension Ramp" and the ROI of the investment.

## 2. Core Objectives
1.  **Visual Proof**: Use charts to show the difference between "Inercial" (doing nothing) vs "Target Strategy".
2.  **Granularity**: Show the exact math for every single year of the strategy (Salary, Cost, Accumulated Weeks).
3.  **Context**: Explain *why* a specific year makes the pension jump (e.g., crossing the 2000 weeks threshold or changing avg salary).

## 3. Key Metrics to Expose
-   **Projection Timeline**: 5-Year forward look.
-   **Investment Flow**: Monthly payment vs Accumulated Capital.
-   **Pension Growth**: How the pension amount increases with eaech added year of investment.
-   **ROI Break-even**: Exact date when the pension delta pays off the investment.

## 4. Interaction Model
-   **Modal-First**: The user clicks "Ver Detalle" and a high-density information modal appears. They never leave the context of the Dashboard.
-   **Interactive Charts**: Hovering over the chart updates the summary cards.
