# B2B2C SaaS Ecosystem & Advisor Marketplace (STRAT-032)

## Executive Summary
This document formalizes the pivot of the Retiro Maestro platform from a direct-to-consumer (B2C) calculator into a comprehensive B2B2C ecosystem (Business-to-Business-to-Consumer). The platform will serve both end-users seeking basic orientation and independent financial advisors (Advisors) who utilize the platform's advanced mathematical engines as a SaaS to service those users.

## The Dual-Persona Funnel
The platform operates on a two-sided marketplace flow:
1.  **The Origin User (B2C):** Users who are evaluating their retirement options. They enter the platform at a **Free Tier** to obtain a basic diagnostic (e.g., 1 simple simulation per week) and seek a human connection.
2.  **The Sovereign Advisor (B2B):** Independent consultants operating under the **GOLD Tier** (or similar premium subscriptions). They process the Origin Users' cases, generate full detailed strategies, and act as the legal/consulting layer. 

## Business Logic Segregation
The core distinction lies in revenue generation contexts:
*   **Platform Revenue (SaaS Engine):** The platform generates revenue by charging Advisors for the GOLD subscription (access to the simulation engine, CRM portfolio, and forensic auditing) and through Pay-Per-Request modules (e.g., retrieving official IMSS reports).
*   **Advisor Revenue (Independent Consulting):** The platform *does not* intermediate the consulting fees. Advisors negotiate and collect their honorariums independently from the clients they service via the platform's outputs.

## Strategic Objectives
1.  **Matchmaking:** Enable Origin Users to find or be assigned to an Advisor.
2.  **Portfolio Management (CRM):** Provide Advisors with a dedicated dashboard to manage multiple clients, view their dossiers, and run advanced projections on their behalf.
3.  **Data Segregation & Privacy:** Ensure that an Advisor can only access the dossiers of Users explicitly assigned to them, maintaining strict data sovereignty.
