# 🎯 Strategy: STRAT-004 Forensic Audit & Immutable Ledger

## Mission Statement
To **ensure legal auditability and high-trust evidence** for **Mexican workers** by **implementing an immutable ledger of all pension projections**, enabling **forensic verification of calculated rights** while adhering to **Protocol 13 (Signed Intent) and Protocol 23 (Legacy)**.

## Wardley Map Reference
* **Strategic Anchor**: **Legal Certainty**.
* **Differentiator**: Cryptographically signed proof of projection that can be presented in labor court.
* **Evolution**: Moving "Calculation Logs" from a Transient Session (Custom) to a "Permanent Evidence Vault" (Commodity).

## Justificación Técnica y Económica
* **Cynefin Classification**: **Complicated**. Requires stable cryptographic primitives and durable storage patterns.
* **Build vs. Buy**: **Génesis Build**. Commercial calculators do not provide signed evidence or local-first immutable logs.
* **ROI Analysis**: 
    * **Legal Leverage**: Provides the worker with a precise, timestamped record of what anchors (UMA/SMDF) and logic (Article 167) were used, preventing IMSS from arbitrarily changing the "Ground Truth" during a resolution.
    * **Audit Efficiency**: One-click export of a "Forensic Dossier."

## Protocol 23: Legacy (Exodus Plan)
* **Succession Kit**: Export of the entire ledger to Apache Parquet for long-term analytical durability.
* **Sovereign Keys**: User-controlled signing keys (local-first) to ensure the ledger cannot be tampered with by a central authority.
