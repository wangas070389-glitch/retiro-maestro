# ADR-020: SaaS Monetization, Entitlements & B2B2C Revenue Model

## Status
ACCEPTED

## Context
The platform has pivoted from a direct-to-consumer (B2C) calculator to a **B2B2C SaaS ecosystem** (STRAT-032, POST-010). This fundamentally changes the monetization strategy: the primary **Willingness to Pay (WTP)** is not the individual retiree, but the **independent financial Advisor** who uses the engine to scale their consulting practice.

Additionally, the Landing Page CRO audit (STRAT-033) revealed that "Sovereign" and "Zero-Knowledge" branding creates friction with the target demographic (workers age 55+). The monetization touchpoints must shift to benefit-driven language: "Precisión Actuarial", "Ley 73", "Certidumbre".

## Decision
Integrate **Stripe** as the payment and entitlement engine under a **B2B2C-aware tier model**.

### 1. Tier Definitions (Implemented — ADR-023)
| Tier | Primary Persona | Features |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **FREE** | End-User (Consumer) | Basic simulations; in-memory only; standard PDF report; limited to 1 simulation/week. Entry point for organic lead generation. |
| **GOLD** | Financial Advisor (Power User) | Cloud persistence; Sovereign Forensic Dossier; OptimizerEngine access; M40 Smart Calendar; Oracle Pulse sync; multi-client CRM portfolio. |
| **SOVEREIGN** | Enterprise Advisor / Institution | All GOLD features + full Legal Document Suite (Alta M40, Baja Patrón templates); multi-device sync; priority support. |

### 2. B2B2C Revenue Logic
- **Platform Revenue**: Charged to **Advisors** (GOLD/SOVEREIGN subscriptions) for access to the actuarial SaaS engine, CRM portfolio, and forensic auditing.
- **Advisor Revenue**: Retiro Maestro does **not** intermediate the consulting honorariums between Advisors and their clients. This relationship is independent.
- The **FREE tier** serves as an organic funnel: end-users enter for a basic diagnostic and are matched or referred to an Advisor for full-service planning.

### 3. Entitlement Enforcement
- **Server-Side Verification**: The `session.user.tier` field (mapped from the DB via NextAuth) is validated on every sensitive server action. Client-side state is never trusted.
- **`SubscriptionGate` HOC**: Applied at the route level for `/authority` advanced features and the full Dossier module. Returns `FORBIDDEN` with a premium upgrade CTA (Zona 3 of BLUE-033).
- **Stripe Integration**: Manages subscription lifecycle (create, update, cancel) and webhooks update the `tier` field in the `User` table.

### 4. Legal & Compliance Guardrails
- **IVA Compliance**: Stripe handles Mexican VAT automatically — critical for a legal-adjacent product.
- **Legal Disclaimer**: Every report and subscription confirmation must display: *"Retiro Maestro es una herramienta de simulación actuarial, no un despacho de asesoría legal o financiera regulada."*
- **Tax Integrity**: Reports must always separate Gross Pension (before ISR) from Net Pension (after ISR) to prevent misleading projections.

### 5. Conversion Language
Per STRAT-033 and STRAT-034, all user-facing monetization copy must avoid: "Sovereign", "Zero-Knowledge", "Ledger Forense", "Criptográfico". Replace with: "Precisión Actuarial", "Ley 73 Verificado", "Seguridad de Datos".

## Consequences
- **Positive**: Higher WTP alignment — Advisors monetize the tool through their clients' honorariums, creating strong business model alignment.
- **Positive**: Stripe handles IVA, subscription proration, and card management globally.
- **Negative**: Introduces Stripe as a 3rd-party dependency; mitigated by keeping it in the payment layer only (engine remains sovereign).
- **Risk**: Feature-gate frustration for FREE users. Mitigated by a generous FREE tier baseline and clear, honest pricing (STRAT-033, Rec 8).

## Superseded Concepts
- **"Enterprise: Multi-coworker dossiers"** → Replaced by the **Advisor CRM model** (STRAT-032, ADR-034) which uses the Polymorphic User Model's `advisorId` relation.
- **"Professional"** tier renamed to **"GOLD"** (ADR-023).
- **"Sovereign Free"** tier renamed to **"FREE"** for user-facing clarity (STRAT-033).
